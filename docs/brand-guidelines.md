# ClassVybes Brand Guidelines

## Brand idea

ClassVybes makes revision feel like a track worth returning to. The brand lives where a marked-up lecture note meets a collectable record sleeve.

## Creative north star

**The Annotated Mixtape.** Academic content remains visible and credible, while rhythm, cover art, typography, and motion make repetition feel culturally alive.

## Brand promise

Turn the key ideas in your notes into study tracks you can replay, recall, and check.

## Personality

- **Rhythmic:** Pace, repetition, and sequence shape the experience.
- **Sharp:** Copy is specific, hierarchy is clear, and claims are disciplined.
- **Encouraging:** The brand helps learners return to difficult material without shame or pressure.

## Voice

Write like a thoughtful senior student who understands music, not like an institution or a trend-chasing app.

### Do

- Use concrete verbs: upload, choose, review, replay, remember.
- Keep sentences short enough to scan on a phone.
- Name real product limits and unresolved policies.
- Use music language where it clarifies the product.
- Speak to one learner in the hero.

### Do not

- Use forced slang or mimic a specific artist's voice.
- Use claims such as "learn twice as fast" or "scientifically proven."
- Use generic AI language such as revolutionize, unleash, or next-generation.
- Describe ClassVybes as magic.
- Shame users through streaks, scarcity, or exam fear.

## Naming and tagline

The wordmark is **ClassVybes**, with a capital C and V. Keep the spelling consistent. Do not alternate with Class Vibes, Classvybes, or CLASSVYBES in body copy.

Primary tagline: **Your notes. Your sound.**

Functional explanation: **Turn your notes into study tracks.**

## Logo treatment

The launch site uses a confident typographic wordmark until a formal vector mark is designed. Keep clear space equal to the height of the capital C. Never place the wordmark over a visually busy image without a solid backing. Do not create a generic musical-note logo, graduation-cap icon, or AI sparkle mark.

## Color

The color strategy is **Committed**. Cobalt carries major surfaces and compositions. Signal chartreuse is rare and functional. Pure neutrals keep study content clear.

- **Deep Current:** `oklch(0.550 0.105 230)` anchors brand surfaces, links, and structural emphasis.
- **Open Cobalt:** `oklch(0.620 0.160 230)` carries large color fields and active controls.
- **Signal Chartreuse:** `oklch(0.860 0.190 122)` marks the primary call to action, active genre, and lyric focus only.
- **Ink:** `oklch(0.155 0.020 240)` is the primary light-theme text.
- **White:** `oklch(1.000 0.000 0)` is the light canvas.
- **Mist Surface:** `oklch(0.965 0.012 230)` separates quiet content groups.
- **Night:** `oklch(0.105 0.018 240)` is the dark canvas.
- **Night Surface:** `oklch(0.165 0.026 235)` separates dark-theme content groups.

**The Signal Rule.** Chartreuse appears on less than ten percent of a screen. It must mean action, selection, or learning focus.

**The One-World Rule.** Every section stays inside the cobalt, chartreuse, and neutral system. No isolated purple, orange, or pink section themes.

## Typography

### Display

**Ojuju Variable** provides a distinctive reverse-contrast voice with roots in African masquerade forms. Use it for short, high-impact headlines and track titles. Keep display lines brief, left aligned, and carefully tested at mobile widths.

### Body and interface

**Inclusive Sans Variable** handles body copy, navigation, labels, controls, and data. Its open counters and non-mirrored forms support accessibility without feeling clinical.

### Rules

- Maximum hero size is 6rem with letter spacing no tighter than -0.035em.
- Body copy stays between 16px and 20px with a maximum width of 68ch.
- Ojuju does not appear in paragraphs, form labels, or safety copy.
- Labels use sentence case. Uppercase is reserved for one short hero support line.
- No monospace family. ClassVybes is a learning and music brand, not a developer tool.

## Imagery

Photography should feel observed, not staged. Show real study material, phones, headphones, campus movement, public transit, and everyday light. Casting should reflect the launch audience without turning Nigerian identity into props or patterns.

Cover art uses tactile collage, rhythm-led geometry, photocopied paper texture, and one strong composition per genre. Never use AI robots, holographic brains, neon purple people, generic graduation images, or hand-drawn doodles.

## Shape

Surfaces use a controlled 14px radius. Inputs use 10px. Interactive buttons may use a full pill when their silhouette helps scanning. Do not apply oversized 24-40px rounding to sections or cards.

## Motion

Motion communicates transformation, sequence, and selection. It does not exist as background spectacle.

- Notes become lyric lines in a single scroll story.
- Media moves at a restrained parallax range on desktop.
- Genre artwork changes only after direct user input.
- Sound never autoplays.
- Reduced-motion mode removes pinning, parallax, scrub, and automatic movement.

## Brand anti-pattern test

If the page could be recolored purple and sold as any AI tool, the identity has failed. If it could be recolored blue and sold as any study app, the story has failed. The finished page must be recognizable through type, rhythm, imagery, and the source-to-track narrative before the logo is read.
