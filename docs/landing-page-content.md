## Transformation story

**Headline:** From heavy notes to a repeatable track.

**Intro:** ClassVybes follows a simple path from source material to reviewable study music.

### Bring the material

Upload a PDF, document, image, or pasted summary. Your source stays at the center of the process.

### Shape the sound

Choose a genre, voice, tempo, and learning mode that fit the topic and your listening habits.

### Review and replay

Check the lyrics, save the track, and return to the idea whenever revision fits your day.

**Timing note:** The product target for full track generation is 2-5 minutes.

## Features

**Headline:** Built around the way a track becomes familiar.

- Synchronized lyrics keep words and sound together.
- Loop controls repeat the part that still needs work.
- Playlists organize tracks by subject and course.
- Karaoke mode turns listening into active recall.
- Downloads keep revision available beyond a stable connection.
- Progress views show habits without shaming missed days.

## Safety

**Headline:** Keep the source in view.

**Body:** Generated lyrics should be easy to review, edit, and compare with your material. ClassVybes is a study aid, not a replacement for the original source.

### Accuracy

Important concepts should link back to the passage that produced them.

### Privacy

Upload use, retention, deletion, and model-training choices must be stated before launch.

### Responsible music

The product should avoid artist impersonation, cloned celebrity voices, and unsupported copyright claims.

## FAQ

### What can I upload?

The MVP is planned to support PDF, DOCX, TXT, images with text recognition, and pasted text.

### Which genres are planned?

The PRD includes Afrobeats, Hip-Hop, Amapiano, Pop, Gospel, Classical, Jazz, and Lo-Fi.

### How long will a track take?

The product target is 2-5 minutes for a full generated track. Actual performance must be verified before launch.

### Can I trust every generated lyric?

AI output can be inaccurate. ClassVybes should make review and source comparison part of the normal flow.

### Will my notes be used to train models?

That policy has not been finalized. The launch product must answer it clearly before accepting uploads.

### Is ClassVybes a replacement for studying?

No. It adds music-led recall and repetition to the study methods that already work for you.
